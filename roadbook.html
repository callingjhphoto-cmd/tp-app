🚦 Conditions

2026 is an above-average snow year, but only above ~2,300 m — your whole route is below ~2,000 m, so no snow closures. The real flags:

⛽ Aragón fire level (NAPIF) — check before noon each day; Rojo/Rojo+ can shut forest tracks (aragon.es)
🚫 Andorra — foreign-plate ban on its natural tracks (a Spanish plate is foreign there). Skip Andorra dirt; tarmac through for fuel only
🦌 Boumort — some sectors are guide-only this season; call the reserve before relying on a track
⚖️ Navarra — restrictive: stay strictly on legal pistas

★ OPTIMISED — deep-research verdict (4 agents)

✅ LEGAL — settled: the Alt Pirineu motor restriction is seasonal (1 Nov–31 May). You ride 8–12 June — it's lifted. The Smugglers' Route (Alins–Tor–Cabús) is legal for your dates. Ride it.
✅ TRAIL: Navarra isn't "closed" — the TET-Spain Navarra line is legal & ~90% dirt, ride it. Best gravel of the trip = Hecho → Selva de Oza → Guarrinza/Agua Tuerta. Boumort = optional (game reserve, may be gated — verify on the day). Don't detour to Sierra de Guara (bicycle terrain).
⛽ FUEL: T7 never runs dry on these days. Ansó & Ochagavía have NO fuel. Fill Hecho (before 14:00 — single point of failure Thu) + Urzainqui (last before Belagua). Carry a 3–5 L bottle for the Hecho→Isaba leg. Cheapest fuel = Esclatoil, La Seu d'Urgell ~€1.42 — brim it; skip Andorra. Total ≈ €60–80.
🍴 FOOD upgrades: START with a Costa Brava seafood lunch (Restaurant Costa Brava, Sa Riera, Begur). Aínsa: dropped the weak picks → Casa Falceto (4.6★, value) or Callizo (1★ Michelin, splurge). Buy Roncal cheese at Quesos Larra, Burgui. San Sebastián order: Néstor (reserve first) → La Cuchara de San Telmo → Gandarias → Borda Berri. Borda Chiquín (Ansó) = weekend/daytime only.
📸 CONTENT/vistas added: Mallos de Riglos (sunset), Congost de Mont-rebei (detour), Ordesa miradors (Añisclo), Foz de Lumbier + Arbayún (vultures), Belagua/Larra, Irati (Pikatua), finish at Cabo Higuer lighthouse at sunset. All pinned in the GPX.

📞 Operational answers (resolved 7 Jun)

⛽ Hecho pump (Moeve, A-176): open 07:00–14:00 / 16:00–20:00, 7 days. Fill before 14:00 Thursday. Doubt? Call 974 375 321. It's the ONLY pump for Hecho+Ansó.
🦌 Boumort: private bikes CAN legally use the public tracks (no permit). Call 973 654 716 to confirm your track is open + not in an authorised-only zone.
🏔️ Ordesa: the mandatory-shuttle restriction starts ~19–20 June — you're before it, so you can ride up to La Pradera (go early, car park can fill).
🚧 Closures: none block 8–12 June. A-176 Ansó works ended in May; Belagua open by June. Check the morning you ride high passes: Aragón visincar.aragon.es · Catalonia transit.gencat.cat (Coll de Pal — highest, confirm) · Navarra 900 400 100.
